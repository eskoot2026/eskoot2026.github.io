About Eskoot

Building safer streets
for everyday riders.

Eskoot is an edge-AI safety platform designed to protect micromobility riders. Our hardware modules detect road hazards like potholes, cracks, and obstacles in real time—alerting riders before danger becomes an accident.

Our Mission

We believe micromobility should be safe by default. Eskoot exists to reduce preventable accidents by bringing real-time intelligence directly onto e-scooters and bikes—without relying on cloud latency or user attention.

Why We Built Eskoot

Potholes and road defects are one of the leading causes of micromobility injuries. Existing solutions react too late. Eskoot predicts danger ahead of time using on-device vision and low-latency alerts.

What We Value

Safety First

Every design decision is driven by rider safety, reliability, and trust.

Edge Intelligence

Real-time decisions happen on the device—no delays, no dependency on connectivity.

Scalability

Built for mass deployment across fleets, cities, and diverse road environments.

Our Vision

We envision cities where micromobility is as safe and trusted as public transit. By turning every ride into a data-informed experience, Eskoot helps riders, operators, and cities identify risk and build smarter infrastructure.
