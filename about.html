About Us

Eskoot is a mobility safety team focused on reducing accidents in e-scooters and micromobility through real-time, on-device hazard detection. We believe safer streets start with better awareness. Using real-time terrain analysis, our module detects micro-hazards and delivers non-intrusive haptic alerts, allowing riders to react before accidents happen.
Join us in bringing millions of e-scooter riders to their home safely!

Our Mission

Real-time rider safety

Provide a safer e-scooter riding environment in all conditions by actively detecting road hazards and warning riders in real time.

Growing hazard intelligence

Build a continuously growing hazard dataset that helps shared e-scooter operators better understand and reduce road risks.

Impact at city scale

Deliver Eskootâ€™s safety module to large e-scooter companies worldwide, creating meaningful safety improvements across global cities.

We envision Eskoot as a solution that fundamentally improves safety in micromobility. By reducing crashes caused by poor road conditions, Eskoot helps make e-scooter transportation safer, more reliable, and more widely accepted in urban environments.

Safety and sustainability are core values of our mission. Eskoot continuously reuses and learns from collected hazard data to improve performance over time.

In the long term, our goal is to enable safer infrastructure by providing a platform where shared e-scooter companies and municipalities gain a deeper understanding of road conditions.
