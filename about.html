About Us

Eskoot is a mobility safety company focused on reducing accidents in e-scooters and micromobility through real-time, on-device hazard detection. We believe safer streets start with better awareness.

Eskoot began with a simple observation: most scooter accidents aren’t caused by reckless riding, but by invisible road conditions. Potholes, cracks, and uneven pavement appear suddenly — especially at night.

We set out to build a compact, affordable module that could detect these hazards instantly and warn riders before it’s too late.
