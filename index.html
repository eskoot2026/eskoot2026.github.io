Our modules detect potholes, cracks, and road hazards in real time and alerts riders instantly.
Join us in bringing millions of e-scooter riders to their home safely!

Every day, more and more electric scooter riders are sent to emergency rooms - not by reckless speed, but the tiniest cracks and shallow potholes they never even see coming. These micro-terrain hazards are now the leading causes of accidents, contributing to injuries surging roughly 600% over the past seven years and leaving a critical gap in our public safety net.

Eskoot is a ground-breaking AI-powered safety module designed specifically to close this gap. Using real-time terrain analysis, Eskoot detects micro-hazards and delivers non-intrusive haptic alerts, allowing riders to react before accidents happen. Moreover, it aggregates hazard data into a safety analytics platform used to identify high-risk locations and optimise operations. Eskoot serves to not only promise safer rides but also build a safer micromobility ecosystem and ensure innovation does not come at the cost of human safety.
