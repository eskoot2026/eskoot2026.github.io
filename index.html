Hi. We're Eskoot (pronounced Eh-Scoot), the only end-to-end safety service for micromobility.
Our modules detect potholes, cracks, and road hazards in real time and alerts riders instantly.
Join us in bringing millions of e-scooter riders to their home safely!

A routine e-scooter trip for Han-ki ended with an ambulance trip, multiple forearm fractures, and 2 weeks in a hospital bed. Han-ki wasn’t going at breakneck speeds, nor did he take a risky detour. Instead, all it took was hitting a single pothole. This may be Han-ki's tale, but his story is one shared by millions more. Every year, more than 210,000 e-scooter riders end up in emergency rooms, with injury numbers increasing over 600% for the last 7 years. This makes e-scooters one of the fastest-growing public safety concerns today.

Using state-of-the-art terrain analysis technology, Eskoot's AI-powered module detects micro-terrain hazards in real-time to deliver intuitive haptic alerts directly to the handlebars, giving riders the split-second reaction time they need. Beyond protecting individual riders, Eskoot generates a comprehensive hazard database that helps operators build safer infrastructure. Join us in bringing millions of riders safely home!
(This testimonial was approved by Han-ki Cho, Pangyo High School)
